6 Incredible Sports to Watch

From the high-speed thrills of alpine skiing to the strategic precision of wheelchair curling, discover every sport at Milano Cortina 2026.

⛷️

Para Alpine Skiing

High-speed downhill races through the stunning Cortina d'Ampezzo slopes. Events include Downhill, Super-G, Giant Slalom, Slalom, and Super Combined.

Learn More →
🎯

Para Biathlon

The ultimate test of endurance and accuracy, combining cross-country skiing with precision rifle shooting across stunning Italian trails.

Learn More →
🎿

Para Cross-Country Skiing

Athletes push their limits across challenging Nordic courses, competing in sprint, mid-distance, long-distance, and relay events.

Learn More →
🏒

Para Ice Hockey

Fast, physical, and electrifying team sport played on sledges. One of the most popular events at every Winter Paralympics.

Learn More →
🏂

Para Snowboard

Athletes carve through banked slalom and race head-to-head in snowboard cross — a crowd favorite with incredible energy.

Wheelchair Curling

Strategic brilliance on ice. This year features the debut of mixed doubles alongside the traditional team event.
