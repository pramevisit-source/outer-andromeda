  • Your IP address

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

We use cookies for:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at pramevisit@gmail.com.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:
